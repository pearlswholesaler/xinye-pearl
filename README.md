## 🏭 About Us

**CHANGZHOU XINYE PEARL CRAFTS FACTORY** — established in **2005**, located in Changzhou, Jiangsu, China.

We are a direct pearl manufacturer and wholesale supplier specializing in **freshwater pearls** — from raw loose pearls and strands to fully finished jewelry. With over **1,000 varieties** in stock, we serve retailers, designers, and brands worldwide with consistent quality and factory-direct pricing.
